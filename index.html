About Me

I am a Carnegie Postdoctoral Fellow at the Carnegie Observatories in Pasadena, CA. My interests include radio observations of star-forming galaxies, statistical analyses of faint populations, modeling the cosmological evolution of galaxies, and more. Prior to arriving in Pasadena, I received my PhD from the Department of Astronomy at the University of Virginia in Summer 2021 and my undergraduate degrees in Physics and Mathematics from Lafayette College.
